About SilentSpeak

Breaking Communication
Barriers with AI

SilentSpeak uses two state-of-the-art pre-trained models running entirely in your browser to translate sign language and lip movements into text in real time.

✋

Sign Language Recognition

Uses MediaPipe HandPose to extract 21 3D hand landmarks per frame, feeding them into a trained gesture classifier that maps hand shapes and movements to ASL signs and phrases.

MediaPipe HandsTF.jsLSTM
👄

Lip Reading AI

MediaPipe FaceMesh extracts 478 facial landmarks with special focus on 40 lip landmarks. A sequence model reads lip shapes over time to predict spoken words without audio.

FaceMeshTF.jsGRU
⚡

Combined Fusion

In Combined mode, both models run simultaneously. A confidence-weighted fusion layer picks the highest-certainty output, giving you the best of both worlds in one seamless stream.

Dual StreamFusion LayerWebGL
🔒

100% Private

All inference runs locally in your browser using WebGL acceleration. No video, audio, or biometric data is ever sent to any server. Your communication stays yours.

On-DeviceWebGLZero Upload
